## Never lose your data again

CrownServer Cloud Backup protects all your important data automatically. Computers, smartphones, tablets: everything is backed up continuously.

### Automatic and invisible

Once configured, the backup runs automatically without you having to think about it. Your devices are protected 24/7.

### Quick recovery

If you lose your data, restoring is simple and fast. You can recover the entire system or just specific files, from any point in time.
